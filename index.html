Register now for the Southern Regional Meeting of the American Physician Scientists Association!

Register Now!


Registration deadline is September 2nd!

The Texas A&M College of Medicine M.D./Ph.D program invites you to the 2019 APSA Southern Regional Meeting in College Station, TX on Saturday, September 7th, 2019. This meeting is open to prospective and current medical and dual-degree students, residents, fellows, and faculty. Join us in engaging with a series of scientific and career-oriented talks from distinguished faculty and guests, student poster and oral presentation sessions, and small-group discussions facilitated by students and faculty to address issues relevant to all levels of training.
We look forward to meeting you, hearing about your scientific pursuits and career path, and hope you enjoy a day of meeting and sharing experiences with current and hopeful physician scientists!
